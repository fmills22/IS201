About Me

The Face behind the Recipes

My name is Felicity Mills. It really should be Felicity Ekins as I was married last summer, however I'm putting off making the official change until my passport expires. I'm currently a student at BYU, planning on applying to the Experience Design and Management program in June with the hope of one day becoming a coorporate event planner. If that doesn't work out, I suppose I could fall on the sword and do HR.

I met my husband when I was 15 years old. He had just moved to Utah from Virginia and we both had gotten a summer job at a shaved ice shack. We became good friends and started dating a few years later. We were married on August 18, 2023. He reminds me of Pa from the Little House on the Prairie. Soft spoken with a heart of gold.

Although I've been baking my whole life, I didn't really get into cooking until I got married. Baking bread is my love language. My husband and I have no children, but we do have a bouncing baby sourdough starter. Many of the recipes shared on this site are third or fourth generation recipes. I was lucky enough to grow up baking with my grandmothers and they taught me everything I know.
